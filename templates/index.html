How it works

01
Preprocess
Text is lowercased, URLs/handles stripped, stop words removed, and words lemmatized.
02
Vectorize
TF-IDF with bigrams converts the cleaned text into a numeric feature vector.
03
Classify
Three models — Logistic Regression, SVM, and Random Forest — each cast a vote.
04
Result
Each model returns a class and confidence score. The majority vote is the final verdict.
